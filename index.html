Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency.

Bitcoin was the first cryptocurrency to successfully record
transactions on a secure, decentralized blockchain-based network.
Launched in early 2009 by its pseudonymous creator Satoshi Nakamoto,
Bitcoin is the largest cryptocurrency measured by market
capitalization and amount of data stored on its blockchain.
